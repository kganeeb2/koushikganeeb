About

Mr. Koushik Kumar Ganeeb is an Engineering Leader at Salesforce, building AI revolution and advancing Engineering Capabilities in the industry. Since May 2017, he has been a Principal Member of Technical Staff at Salesforce's Data Cloud, designing and implementing Public Cloud Architecture for the Data Factory Service. His work involves managing large-scale data extracts and imports to AWS S3, Azure Blob, and Oracle, developing extraction pipelines that enhance Salesforce Einstein's AI capabilities — processing trillions of transactions daily for Fortune 100 companies.
