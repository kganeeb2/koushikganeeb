About

Koushik Kumar Ganeeb is an Engineering Leader at Salesforce Data Cloud, where he architects distributed systems and AI-powered data pipelines processing trillions of transactions daily for Fortune 100 enterprises. With over 16 years of experience spanning cloud infrastructure, data engineering, and team leadership, he brings deep technical expertise paired with a passion for mentorship and community building.

A recognized thought leader in the global technology community, Koushik holds fellowships from BCS, IETE, SCRS, SAS Society, Hackathon Raptors, and the IOASD, among others. He is a Senior Member of IEEE and serves on more than 20 technical program and review committees worldwide. He also mentors engineers globally through ADPList and organizes the AI Collective at Columbia University.
